Pan-Fried Fish 2023

Item Quantity Preparation Price
Cod or Fish Fillets 2-5 oz 10.00
All-Purpose Flour 1/4 cup 2.19
Old Bay 2 tsp 4.43
Pepper 1 tsp 1.00
Vegetable Oil 1/4 cup 2.39
Total: $20.01
  1. Rinse fish and dry very well.
  2. Mix together seasonings.
  3. Pre-heat fry pan over med heat.
  4. Pour in oil to pan to a depth of ¼ inch
  5. Bread fish by turning into flour until coated. Shake off excess.
  6. Add fish to oil moving pan for ten seconds to keep fish from sticking to bottom.
  7. Cook until golden brown. Drain on paper towel. Serve with tartar sauce.

Tartar sauce can be made with Mayo, diced pickles and or capers, white wine vinegar, coarse mustard and Salt and Black Pepper.

Chips (French Fries) Double fry method

Item Quantity Preperation Price
Vegetable Oil Frying Oil 2.39
Salt to taste 0.79
Potatoes 1-2 ea planks 0.66
Total: $3.84
  1. After cutting potatoes, rinse them well to remove starch. Dry well.
  2. Fry potatoes at 300 degrees for 4 minutes and are still blond in color. Pat dry.
  3. Arrange on sheet pan and refrigerate until they are at least as cold as room temp.
  4. Fry a second time at 360 until crispy and golden brown.

New England Clam Chowder

Item Quantity Preperation Price
Bacon Strips 2 center 3.98
Celery 1 chopped 0.03
Potatoes 1-2 ea planks 0.66
Pepper 1/8 tsp 1.00
Thyme 1/8 tsp 1.94
Whole Garlic Clove 1 ea minced 0.46
All-Purpose Flour 1 tbl 2.19
Clam Juice 4 oz 2.46
Fish Stock 1 cup 3.65
Onion 1/4 small diced 1.19
Milk 2 cup divided 1.87
Clam 6-1/2 oz chopped undrained 2.32
Water 1/2 cup 0.00
Total: $21.75
  1. In a medium pot cook bacon over medium heat until crisp.
  2. Remove to paper towels to drain; set aside.
  3. Sauté celery and onion in the drippings until tender.
  4. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer.
  5. Stir in the potatoes, water, clam juice, stock, pepper and thyme.
  6. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  7. In a small bowl, combine flour and 1/2 cup Milk until smooth.
  8. Gradually stir into soup. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened.
  9. Stir in clams and remaining Milk; heat through (do not boil). Crumble the cooked bacon; sprinkle over each serving.
  10. Hot sauce to taste is optional
